ACADEMICS

Curriculum Overview

At Lumora International School, our curriculum is designed to develop academic excellence, creativity, leadership, innovation, and lifelong learning through a balanced educational approach.

Primary School

Grades 1–5 focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, creativity, communication, and confidence building.

Middle School

Grades 6–8 strengthen analytical thinking, STEM learning, coding, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Senior Secondary

Grades 9–12 prepare students for higher education, competitive exams, leadership, and career readiness.
