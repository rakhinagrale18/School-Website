Admission Process

Step 1

Complete the online admission application form with accurate student information.

Step 2

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous report cards, and photographs.

Step 3

Attend an interaction session or entrance assessment based on the grade level.

Step 4

Receive admission confirmation and complete the fee payment process.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age requirement according to grade level.
  • Previous academic records (where applicable).
  • Valid Birth Certificate.
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Transfer Certificate (for higher classes).
